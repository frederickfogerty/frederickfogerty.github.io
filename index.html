About Me

5 years of experience in technology innovation, product, and entrepreneurship. Currently, as Founder and CTO of JRNY, I am delivering technical solutions for clients such as Volkswagen, NZ Government. I am also proud to be helping the community, by working with non-profits like Youthline. I am fundamentally a people person, and I believe that through good leadership, we can bring people together to achieve greatness far beyond the sum of their talents and passions.

JRNY is the latest step in my entrepreneurial journey, as myself and three others set out on a journey to change the relationship between a company and their consumers. Apart from developing a kick-ass company culture, I have led its technology and product from inception to delivery, either being hands-on or overseeing project design, development, and delivery.

Previously I have worked at companies both large and small. I was always driven to deliver the largest business value possible with the least effort and impact. User Experience is a crucial element of this focus. By focussing on areas such as Front-End Engineering, I can ensure the customer receives the most useful product possible.

Technology is a valuable vehicle to deliver social change, and I believe that through the proper mindset, we can solve many of the world's problems through collaboration and fairness. It is for this reason that I am a technology evangelist, and am eager to aid people in learning technology, by giving talks at events, and helping out wherever I can.
